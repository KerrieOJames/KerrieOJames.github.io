Link to Republication Republication of Claude Schryer's text in visual form using DeivantArt Muro, to illustrate and scribble live in response to sound with the aim to create noisey and loud imagery. The songs are from Montaigne's Album "Glorious Heights" launched in 2016, it is unrelated to the text; merely a tool to aid the expression.
